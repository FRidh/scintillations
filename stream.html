Stream¶

Generate a stream of scintillations. Functions in this module are supposed to work with streaming.stream.

Varying correlation time is taken into account by resampling the generated fluctuations.

scintillations.stream.modulate(signal, fs, nhop, correlation_length, speed, distance, soundspeed, mean_mu_squared, fmin, ntaps_corr=8192, ntaps_spectra=128, window=None, include_saturation=False, state=None, include_amplitude=True, include_phase=True, method='spectra_and_delay')[source]¶

Compute and apply scintillations to signal.

Parameters:
  • signal (Stream) – Signal to modulate.
  • fs – Sample frequency
  • nhop – Compute new impulse response and delay every nhop samples.
  • correlation (Either a single value, or a Stream sampled at fs/nhop.) – Correlation length.
  • speed – Transverse speed.
  • distance (Either a single value, or a Stream sampled at fs/nhop.) – Source-receiver distance.
  • soundspeed (Either a single value, or a Stream sampled at fs/nhop.) – Speed of sound.
  • mean_mu_squared (Either a single value, or a Stream sampled at fs/nhop.) – Variance of the refractive-index field.
  • ntaps_corr – Length of the impulse responses for the correlation filter.
  • ntaps_spectra – Length of the impulse responses for the eventual magnitude-filter.
  • window – Window
  • include_saturation – Whether to include log-amplitude saturation.
  • state – State of the PRNG.
  • include_amplitude – Whether to include log-amplitude fluctuations.
  • include_phase – Whether to include phase fluctuations.
  • method – Method to use.
