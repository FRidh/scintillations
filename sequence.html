Sequence¶

Generate a sequence of scintillations.

Note that this implementation takes into account varying correlation time by updating the impulse response, and not resampling.

scintillations.sequence.covariance_gaussian(spatial_separation, distance, wavenumber, scale, mean_mu_squared)[source]¶

Calculate the covariance of a Gaussian turbulence spectrum and spherical waves.

See Daigle, 1987: equation 2 and 3.

Parameters:
  • spatial_separation – Spatial separation.
  • distance – Distance.
  • wavenumber – Wavenumber.
  • mean_mu_squared – Mean mu squared.
  • scale – Outer length scale.
Returns:

Covariance
\[B{\chi} (\rho) = B{S}(\rho) = \frac{\sqrt{\pi}}{2} \langle \mu^2 \rangle k^2 r L \frac{\Phi(\rho/L) }{\rho / L}\]
